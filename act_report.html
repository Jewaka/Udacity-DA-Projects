Insight #2: What is the association between retweet count and favorite count?¶

We determine whether there is a relatonship between favorited tweets count and retweet count.

We apply Pearson's correlation coefficient formula which returns a value of 0.7 that suggests a fairly strong positive association between the two variables.

A scatter plot also dispays this notable relationship.
